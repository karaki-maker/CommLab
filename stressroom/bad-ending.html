DISTRACTED: BAD ENDING

Inbox, Unopened

Your classes kept posting. The stream kept growing. You told yourself you’d just check one more thing.

Deadlines Pass

Projects turned into zeros. “Missing.” “Late.” The gradebook filled with warnings you avoided opening.

People Notice

Your professor is disappointed you never reached out. Your parents ask if you’re okay. You feel smaller every day you don’t reply.

The Lesson

You didn’t even try. Not once. Maybe next time you’ll choose five focused minutes over five more distractions.

Try again?
